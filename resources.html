Resources

Get Help While in the Life and Help Getting Out


Aurora Commons

https://www.auroracommons.org/

If you would like a place to go where you feel safe and connected to community, Aurora Commons is a wonderful resource!

  • Cook and Share a Meal
  • Foot and Wound Care Clinic
  • Women's Programming
  • S.H.E. Clinic
  • Art Program

Organization for Prostitution Survivors

https://seattleops.org/

If you are looking to get out of the life and want survivor services, OPS has some wonderful resources to help!

  • One-on-one support from an advocate
  • Community Meals
  • Healing Yoga
  • Support Groups
  • Housing Resources

Real Escape from the Sex Trade

https://iwantrest.com/

If you want support or are looking to get out of the life immediately and need emergency services and housing, REST has great resources!

  • 24/7 Emergency Hotline
  • Emergency Shelter
  • Drop-In Center
  • Community Advocates
  • Transitional Housing

Seattle YWCA

https://www.ywcaworks.org/

If you are currently want community support, essencial resources, and a safe place to go, YWCA has great resources!

  • Angeline's Day Center
  • Emergency Housing
  • Domestic Violence Shelter
  • Peer Support for Women Living with HIV
  • Employment Services
